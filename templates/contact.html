Frequently Asked Questions

What is SentimentAI?

SentimentAI is an advanced sentiment analysis platform that uses machine learning to analyze text and determine emotional tone, helping businesses understand customer feedback better.

How accurate is the sentiment analysis?

Our AI models achieve over 95% accuracy in sentiment detection, trained on millions of data points across various industries and languages.

What languages does SentimentAI support?

We currently support over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and many more.

Can I integrate SentimentAI with my existing tools?

Yes! We offer REST APIs, webhooks, and integrations with popular platforms like Slack, Discord, and major CRM systems.

What pricing plans are available?

We offer flexible pricing from free tier for small projects to enterprise plans with custom features. Contact us for a demo and custom quote.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with GDPR and SOC 2 standards, and never share your data with third parties.
